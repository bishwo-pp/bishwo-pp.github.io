Research Summary

Focus: Agentic AI • Media Forensics • Adversarial ML • Multimodal Forensic Pipelines

My research aims to strengthen the integrity of intelligent systems by building agentic AI tools for automated forensic analysis and robust models for detecting manipulated or synthesized media. I combine expertise in RF signal analysis, ML-based anomaly detection, and LLM-driven intelligence to design systems capable of triage, provenance checking, and evidence summarization.

  • AutoGuardX — AI-driven RF anomaly detection for keyless vehicle systems.
  • FUSE-VAPT — OSINT + ML for intelligent vulnerability assessment and threat intelligence.
  • Agentic Forensics Assistant — LLM-based pipeline for metadata analysis and automated reporting.
